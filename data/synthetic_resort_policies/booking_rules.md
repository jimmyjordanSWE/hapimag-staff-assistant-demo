# Booking Rules (Synthetic)

## Standard stay

- Minimum stay: 2 nights
- Maximum stay in one reservation: 21 nights
- Advance booking window: up to 12 months

## Room changes

- Same-category room change: allowed until 48 hours before check-in
- Upgrade request: allowed, subject to availability and rate difference

## Late arrival

- Arrival after 22:00 requires pre-notification
- No-show after midnight without notice triggers no-show handling workflow
