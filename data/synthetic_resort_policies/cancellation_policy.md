# Cancellation Policy (Synthetic)

## Flexible rate

- Free cancellation until 72 hours before check-in
- Cancellation within 72 hours: charge first night

## Member package rate

- Free cancellation until 14 days before check-in
- Cancellation inside 14 days: 50% of package value

## Force majeure guideline

- Escalate to duty manager
- Document reason and supporting evidence
- Manager decides exception outcome and logs rationale
