# Resort Operations SOP (Synthetic)

## Check-in

1. Verify reservation ID and guest identity.
2. Confirm consent notices and billing details.
3. Issue key and provide property safety briefing.

## Incident escalation

1. Classify severity: low, medium, high.
2. For high severity, call duty manager immediately.
3. Record timeline, actions, and owner in incident log.

## Privacy handling

- Never read full identity document data over phone.
- Do not expose personal details in public channels.
- Use redacted identifiers in internal notes.
